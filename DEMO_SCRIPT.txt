[0:00 — HOOK]
Managing medications and checking drug safety can be confusing and stressful for patients. Med-Companion makes this simple and safe by providing a secure, intelligent, multi-agent assistant to check drug interactions and log daily doses.

[0:20 — WHAT IT IS]
Med-Companion is built for the "Agents for Good" track. It focuses on patient safety, data security, and keeping a human in control.

[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's look at the workflow diagram to see how it works.
First, a user's question goes through our Security Checkpoint. This node automatically checks for hacking attempts and hides personal info like emails and Social Security Numbers.
Once clean, the query goes to the main Orchestrator Agent.
The Orchestrator sends tasks to specialized helper agents: the safety_agent checks drug guidelines, and the scheduler_agent handles medication logs.
Both agents use our Model Context Protocol (MCP) Server to access drug databases and local files securely.
Finally, if the user asks to log a medication, the workflow pauses at the Human-in-the-Loop gate to ask for your confirmation before writing any data.

[2:00 — LIVE DEMO]

Let's look at three quick examples in our playground.
First, we ask: "My name is John Doe, my SSN is 123-45-6789, and my email is john@example.com. Can you tell me the guidelines for Metformin?"
Notice that the SSN and email are scrubbed instantly to protect privacy. The safety agent then successfully fetches the Metformin guidelines.

(pause)

Second, let's test drug interaction: "I am taking Aspirin and Warfarin. Is this safe?"
The safety agent checks the database and alerts us that combining these two medications is high-risk and poses a severe bleeding hazard.

(pause)

Third, let's log a dose: "Can you log that I took 1 pill of Metformin now?"
The scheduler agent stages the log entry, and the workflow pauses. We get a prompt asking for our approval. When we type "yes", the system securely logs the dose in our care file.

[3:00 — SECURITY & MCP]
This project ensures patient data stays private and safe. By keeping a human in the loop for critical actions and separating database access via MCP tools, we prevent unauthorized actions and data leaks.

[3:30 — IMPACT / CLOSE]
Med-Companion helps patients manage their health safely from their own local system. It bridges the gap between static medical databases and daily care. Thank you!
